# Shenandoah Telecommunications (shenandoah-telecommunications)

Shenandoah Telecommunications Company (SHEN), doing business as Shentel, is a publicly traded telecommunications company headquartered in Edinburg, Virginia. For over 100 years, Shentel has provided internet, TV, and phone services to residential and business customers across Virginia, West Virginia, Maryland, Pennsylvania, Kentucky, Delaware, Ohio, and Indiana. Through its Glo Fiber and Glo Fiber Business brands, Shentel provides high-speed fiber optic internet, Ethernet, wavelength services, managed network services, and dark fiber leasing to enterprise and wholesale customers.
